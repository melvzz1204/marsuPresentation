MarSU Receives Unmodified Opinion from COA for FY 2025

Marinduque State University (MarSU) has received an Unmodified Opinion from the Commission on Audit (COA) for Fiscal Year 2025.

In its Independent Auditor’s Report, COA confirmed that the University’s financial statements are fairly presented in all material respects and are in full compliance with applicable accounting standards and government regulations.
