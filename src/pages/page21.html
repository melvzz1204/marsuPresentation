MarSU Delegates Participate in IFERP- ICAKMPET 2026, Highlighting Research Excellence and Global Collaboration

Delegates from Marinduque State University (MarSU) actively participated in the 6th International Conference on Advancing Knowledge from Multidisciplinary Perspectives in Education, Engineering & Technology (ICAKMPET-2026), organized by the Institute for Educational Research and Publication (IFERP)-Academy, MarSU and other Academic Partners, held on January 24–25, 2026 in Cebu City.
