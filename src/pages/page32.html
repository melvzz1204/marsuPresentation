Marinduque State University Advances to 269th in 2026 WURI Rankings

Marinduque State University (MarSU) has been recognized among the Top 500 Innovative Universities for 2026 in the World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI), climbing to 269th place from 330th in 2025.

The university also earned seven Top 100 rankings across innovation categories, including student mobility, industrial application, technology infrastructure, and social impact.
