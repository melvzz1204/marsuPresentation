MarSU Strengthens Global Ties at Canada–Philippines Higher Education Forum

Marinduque State University (MarSU) actively advanced its internationalization agenda by joining the Building Bridges in Higher Education: Canada–Philippines Partnership Forum on March 2, 2026, at The Bayleaf Intramuros, Manila.

Representing MarSU at the forum were President Prof. Diosdado P. Zulueta and Asst. Prof. Jholey Rose Leaño-Lancion, Director of the International Relations Office.
