Marinduque State University Among 30 Philippine HEIs Completing CHED–CBIE Internationalization Leadership Training

Leading Internationalization: Strengthening Institutional Capacity and Canada–Philippines Linkages Training
Jointly organized by CHED and CBIE

Marinduque State University (MarSU) proudly marks another milestone in its internationalization journey as its institutional representatives successfully completed the week-long Leading Internationalization: Strengthening Institutional Capacity and Canada–Philippines Linkages training. MarSU is among only 30 Philippine Higher Education Institutions nationwide selected through a competitive process to participate in this prestigious program organized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Canadian Bureau for International Education (CBIE), with support from Global Affairs Canada under the Indo-Pacific Strategy.
